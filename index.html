Welcome to T3!

The Frinkiac-7 Game of Tic-Tac-Toe!

T3 is a tic-tac-toe (3 Ts...T3...get it?) game which pays homage to Professor John I.Q. Nerdelbaum Frink Jr. and one of his greatest creations which was the Frinkiac-7 mainframe. Click here if you're unfamiliar with his genius.

T3 can be played with friends using the same computer or by yourself as a good way to kill time when the boss isn't looking! All you need to do is sign-in with your account and you're all set! What's that? You don't have an account? No problem! Just click on the 'Sign Up' link in the navigation bar above.
