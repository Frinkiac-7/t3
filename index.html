Welcome to T3!

The Frinkiac-7 Game of Tic-Tac-Toe!

T3 is a tic-tac-toe (3 Ts...T3...get it?) game which pays homage to Professor John I.Q. Nerdelbaum Frink Jr. and one of his greatest creations which was the Frinkiac-7 mainframe. Click here if you're unfamiliar with his genius.

Ways to Play

  1. Take turns with friends using the same computer.
  2. Kill time when the boss isn't looking and play against yourself!

All you need to do is sign-in with your account and you're all set! What's that? You don't have an account? No problem! Just click on the 'Sign Up' link in the navigation bar above.
